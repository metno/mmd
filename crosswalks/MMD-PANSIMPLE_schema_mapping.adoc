== Mapping between  MMD and PANSIMPLE

The mapping below is used as background information to create the XLST sheets in the xref:../xslt[xslt folder]. It can be used to map from PANSIMPLE to MMD.

=== References:

. https://github.com/metno/mmd[MMD]
. https://ws.pangaea.de/schemas/pansimple/pansimple.xsd[PANSIMPLE]
. https://www.dublincore.org/schemas/xmls/qdc/2008/02/11/dc.xsd[DUBLIN CORE]
